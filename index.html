How does it work?

1. Get inspired

Your goal is your inspiration, your dream, and your focus. That’s why it’s incredibly important to always keep that goal at the front of your mind. That’s exactly what Focus Planner does.

Every week before thinking about any action items, you write down your goal front and center so you never forget it.

Keeping this goal in mind will remind you of what drove you out of bed this morning and got you to work.

2. Get focused

You know your goal and you’re inspired to achieve that goal. Now everyday ask yourself this: What are the two biggest things stopping me from achieving my goal? These are your blockers.

Flip those blockers around into action items and focus all of your energy on completing those two action items. Once you’ve finished those, you can start on the next three action items that will move you closer to achieving your weekly goal.

3. Get results

At the end of each week sit down and have a honest self assessment and ask yourself the tough questions.

  • How was your effort?
  • What were your wins?
  • What were your losses?
  • Where can you improve?
